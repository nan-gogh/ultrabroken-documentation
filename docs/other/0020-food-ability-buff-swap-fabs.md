# Food Ability Buff Swap (FABS)

## Overview
Allows you to get roasted food (stackable) with a food effect

## How To
```
1. Cook a meal with the desired effect
2. Go to a NPC who sells/gives roasted food DO NOT SORT (see notes for all locations)
3. Eat the food 
4. Buy the cooked food 
5. Now you have the cooked food with an effect (stackable effect food) 
-You can roast more food and it will automaticially have the effect as long as you still have at least one of the roasted food.
```

## Metadata
**Working versions:** 1.0.0
1.1.0/1.1.1
**Credit:** fabs - May 16, 2023
**Source:** Discord
**Examples:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UurzVka6G9M
**Notes:** These are all the places you can obtain cooked food to perform with FABS:
- 2 at Kara Kara Bazaar (Gerudo)
- 3 at Bedrock Bistro (Eldin)
- 1 at Roasted Banana vendor on Great Plateau, at the gate between the warm and cold area.
- 1 from completing the beginner's course of the Lurelin Water Rally race (South of East Necluda, at Lurelin Village)
