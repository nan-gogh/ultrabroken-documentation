# Weapon Dash GAS

## Overview
Allows for easy weapon GAS by performing a dash attack while its fused zonai device is culled

## How To
```
1. Fuse entangle a battery-consuming zonai device to your equipped shield
2. Fuse the zonai device to a weapon
3. Pause while sprinting forward
4. Buffer drop 4 equipment items, then drop the equipped shield and switch to a different one
5. Unpause while holding forward on the left stick, then immediately attack with your weapon and pause again
6. Hold a material to cancel the attack and unpause
7. Detangle the zonai device from the shield that it is fuse-entangled to
```
