# Ultimate Pocket Rocket

## Overview
A pocket rocket which does not break on warps or drain energy cells, and has unlimited usage and range

## How To
```
1. Fuse entangle a weapon on one durability (with no remaining fuse durability), to a shield
2. Pickup the weapon and map zuggle it near a moblin without putting Link's back to a wall so it falls on the ground
3. Equip a different weapon and smuggle it, ensure no other weapons are equipped
4. Aggro the moblin and pickup the fuse entangled weapon just as the moblin picks it up
5. Send the equipped weapon to equipped throken
6. Fuse entangle a rocket to a different shield and then fuse the rocket to the throken weapon
7. Drop the throken weapon to send it back to the moblin
8. Zuggle the shield the rocket is fuse entangled to, pickup the FE base of the throken weapon, and head to a nearby culling area
9. Equip a weapon and fuse entangle a liftable object to it
10. Drop the weapon into the culling area and attach a material to it with ultrahand
11. Exit the culling area while lifting the entangled object to cull Link, drop the equipped shield
12. Watch a memory to uncull Link
13. Pickup the rocket shield, cull Link again using the liftable object, and watch a memory to uncull
14. Pickup the weapon in the culling area and destroy its fuse
15. Move away from the moblin until it unloads, then warp anywhere
```
